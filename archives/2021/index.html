About me

I am chasing my PhD degree at Zhejiang University, China, from Sept. 2018 (co-supervised by Prof. Peng Cheng, Prof. Mingyang Sun and Prof. Jiming Chen. Currently, I am a visiting PhD student at CISPA Helmholtz Center for Information Security (CISPA), Germany, under the supervision of Prof. Michael Backes and Prof. Yang Zhang. I obtained my bachelor degree in June 2018 from Zhejiang University, China.

My recent research interests include differential privacy, machine learning privacy, and intersecting fields.
