About Me

I am currently an Assistant Professor at Xi’an Jiaotong University. My research interests focus on data security and privacy issues in machine learning applications, including privacy protection and dataset copyright auditing. My vision is to protect the legitimate data rights and interests of every individual in the era of widespread artificial intelligence. If you are interested in these research topics, please feel free to contact me via email.

In December 2023, I received my Ph.D. from Zhejiang University in Hangzhou, China, where I was co-supervised by Prof. Peng Cheng, Prof. Jiming Chen, and Prof. Mingyang Sun. Prior to that, I earned my bachelor’s degree from Zhejiang University in June 2018.

From November 2021 to November 2022, I was a visiting Ph.D. student at the CISPA Helmholtz Center for Information Security in Germany, where I conducted research under the supervision of Prof. Michael Backes, Dr. Zhikun Zhang, and Dr. Yang Zhang.
